Hi! I’m Jean.

I am a PhD student in Management at Columbia Business School.

My research lies at the intersection of entrepreneurship and society. I am especially interested in (1) how socioeconomic backgrounds impact entrepreneurial outcomes and (2) how various entrepreneurs are motivated by their social impact. My work has been published in various academic journals including Management Science, PNAS, and Academy of Management Discoveries.

Prior to starting my PhD, I received my BBA and MS in Strategy from Seoul National University. I grew up in Upstate New York and Seoul, Korea. In my free time, I like to play my guitar, dog sit for friends, and visit art museums in New York City.
